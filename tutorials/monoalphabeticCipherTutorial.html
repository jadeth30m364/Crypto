Monoalphabetic Cipher

Each letter in the alphabet is matched to another letter and all instances of the letter are replaced by the other letter.
For example, with the following alphabet, "hi" would be encrypted as "ha" and "ded" would be decrypted as "lol".

a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z
g f w m y o n h a z c d i k e j s t r x q l v p b u
