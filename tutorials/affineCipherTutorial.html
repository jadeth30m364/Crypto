Affine Cipher

Each letter in the alphabet is assigned a number, with a being 0 and z being 25. Also, the mathematical operation mod is basically giving the remainder of dividing a number by another number.
This isn't important for this game, but for every alphabet in the plaintext, the following operation is performed: ax+b(mod 26) with x representing the letter's number and a being coprime with 26.

To decrypt: The formula for decription is the
a-1(x-b) mod 26
a-1 is the multiplicative inverse of a mod 26. Basically, a*a-1 (mod 26) is 1. A table of all the multiplicative inverses are below.
